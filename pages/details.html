Metallica in concert

Formed in 1981 by Lars Ulrich and James Hetfield, Metallica has redefined heavy metal with their powerful blend of speed, aggression, and deep lyrics. With classics like Master of Puppets and The Black Album, the band has left an indelible mark on music, overcoming tragedies and exploring new sonic directions. With every concert and release, Metallica remains a creative force and an icon of metal, proving that their impact and legacy endure over time.
